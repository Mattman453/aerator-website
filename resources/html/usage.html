1) Power On the Device

On the top of the unit, next to the screen, you will see a power button. Press the button and immediately release. Once the screen flashes on, the unit is powered on, and you can move on to the next step.

If the unit fails to turn on after 5 seconds, check the battery and replace it if necessary. If the device still fails to power on, return the product for a replacement or refund.

2) Lay Device on the Ground

Make sure the buttons are to the right of the screen when looking from handle to footrest.

3) Pull Probe From Right Handle

Place the probe in the ground and press the OK/Power button to collect data. Wait for 5 seconds for the device to perform the necessary tests.

4) Check Output on Screen and Lights

If the green light is on, proceed to aeration. If the yellow light is on, proceed to the next step. If the red light is on, aeration is definitely not recommended.

5) Yellow Light Diagnostic

On the screen, a temperature and moisture level are present. If you know you have warm grass or cool grass, proceed to that specific page to confirm if aeration is recommended. If you don't, head to either page.

On each page is a list of grasses that fall under the category of warm or cool grasses, each having a picture alongside the name. If you see a picture on a page that matches your grass, proceed to check for the acceptable ranges for that type of grass. If they match the text on the display, proceed to aeration.
