Cool-Season Grass Conditions

Kentucky Bluegrass: A cool-season grass that forms dense, lush turf and is known for its ability to spread and repair itself through underground rhizomes. It thrives in full sun but requires frequent watering and well-drained soil. It’s ideal for northern climates and lawns that experience moderate foot traffic.

Fescue: A versatile cool-season grass that performs well in shaded areas and is tolerant of drought and poor soil conditions. It’s known for its fine blades, deep root system, and ability to stay green during cooler months. Fescue is great for low-maintenance lawns and areas with inconsistent sunlight.
