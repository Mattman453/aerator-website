Step-by-step guide to using the Smart Aerator Tool

1) Power on the device

On the top of the unit, next to the screen, you will see a power button. Press the button and immediately release. Once the screen flashes on, the unit is powered on, and you can move on to the next step.

If the unit fails to turn on after 5 seconds, check the battery and replace it if necessary. If the device still fails to power on, return the product to the store for a replacement or refund.

2) Lay device on the ground

Make sure the buttons are to the right of the screen when looking from handle to footrest.
